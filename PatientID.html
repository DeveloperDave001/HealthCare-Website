Benue State Integrated Intelligence Health Management Information System (BIIHMIS)

BIIHMIS Patient Card gives a patient the ability to quickly access care from any hospital within the State. It reduces patient waiting time often occasioned by a fresh registration at each hospital. This process is often cumbersome since Health Information Officers request information such as residence, origin, occupational and other facts about a patient. However with these cards, information is accessible at each hospital that he or she visits thus focusing on clinical interventions to prevent the loss of life or complications since TIME could be a friend or an enemy. It could be the edge to successes or setback to failures or losses.
