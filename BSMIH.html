About BSHIA

The Benue State Health Insurance Agency (BSHIA) was established by Law No. 8 of 2018. The Agency:
• Is a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal;
• May sue and be sued in its corporate name;
• May acquire, hold or dispose of any movable or immovable property for the purpose of its function under this Law.
The principal objectives of the Agency shall be to promote, regulate, supervise and ensure the effective administration of the Benue State Health Insurance Scheme. The aim of BSHIA is to facilitate and accelerate attainment of universal health coverage through promoting, regulating, supervising, implementing and ensuring the effective administration of the Health Insurance Scheme. The Agency is to strategically target the formal and informal sectors of the state by mobilizing all stakeholders to operate in a synergistic manner through a sustainable healthcare financing system that is economically viable, culturally and religiously acceptable to the residents of the State.
