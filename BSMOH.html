Ministry of Health

BSMOH

The Ministry of Health and Human Services in Nigeria has eight Directorates, each of which is led by a director. These cover Administration and Supplies, Health Planning, Research and Statistics, Public Health, Medical Services, Nursing Services, Pharmaceutical Services, Finance and Accounts and Medical Laboratory Services.

The Ministry of Health maintains three health training organizations: the School of Nursing, the School of Midwifery, both in Makurdi, and the School of Health Technology in Agasha. In collaboration with other stakeholders, it creates and implements policies that enhance the national health system for the successful, efficient, equitable and inexpensive provision of healthcare.
