IICT- Institute for Information and Communications Technology Limited

IICT-Institute for Information & Communication Technology has broad spectrum specialization in computer networks and software development IICT recently signed an MOU (July 9, 2024) with the BDIC-Benue Digital Infrastructure Company (BDIC), A Private Limited Liability Company setup by the Benue State Government saddled with the task to generate revenue from services of Digital Assets in healthcare, education, mineral resources, Agtech etc within the State. BDIC aims to earn a PLC status within the next 5 years while shielding investors and partners from the norms of the traditional civil service chief of which is unnecessary bureaucratic tunnels   IICT was awarded the task to build a Unified Health Care Information Management System in Benue State. This would allow interoperability of patient’s data across diverse health based facilities in the state and beyond. This concept is the foundation for health care insurance since there would be an audit trial of health based transactions. The project is to connect 23 General Hospitals, 207 Primary Health Care Centers and not less than a thousand other hospitals and medical related centers like Pharmacies, medical Laboratories etc.
