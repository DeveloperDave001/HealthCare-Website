IICT Page

IICT- Institute for Information and Communication Technology Limited

IICT-Institute for Information & Communication Technology has broad spectrum specialization in computer networks and software development IICT recently signed an MOU (July 9, 2024) with the BDIC-Benue Digital Infrastructure Company.

IICT was awarded the task of a Partner working with the team of innovators at BDIC to design a Unified Health Care Information Management System in Benue State. This would allow interoperability of patient’s data across diverse health based facilities in the state and beyond.

This concept is the foundation for health care insurance since there would be an audit trail of health based transactions. The project is to connect 23 General Hospitals, 207 Primary Health Care Centers and not less than a thousand other hospitals and medical related centers like Pharmacies, medical Laboratories etc.
