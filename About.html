About Us

The BDIC- Benue State Digital Infrastructure Company platform allows a cross connect across diverse facilities. This allows continuation of care irrespective of the traditional hospital or related facility of the patient. This means he or she is not seen as a stranger even at first visit at other hospitals or related facilities within or outside Benue State. Each facility has its own admin class. These groups of people are responsible for the determination of access rights to other employees. User Accounts can be activated, stopped and monitored. It is not the responsibility of staff of BDIC to carry this burden since the number of clients on the platform has the tendency to escalate very easily . In addition to this, BDIC is not preview to the internal recruitment processes and exits at these facilities
